Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Metah4 is a privacy-first search engine. This policy explains what data we collect (very little) and what we never collect.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your search queries — they are proxied to Brave Search but not stored or logged by us
  • Your IP address — the server proxy does not log incoming request IPs
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers of any kind
  • Your location — if you enable local search, your coordinates are processed in your browser and only a city/region label is appended to your query; raw coordinates are never sent to our servers
  • Analytics or behavioral data

Third-party services

  • Brave Search API — your search query is forwarded to Brave. See Brave's privacy policy.
  • Pexels — image searches are forwarded to Pexels. See Pexels' privacy policy.
  • Nominatim / OpenStreetMap — if local search is enabled, a reverse geocoding request is made from your browser to Nominatim using your coordinates. See OSM's privacy policy.
  • DuckDuckGo favicon service — small favicon images for result domains are fetched from DuckDuckGo's CDN.

Fonts and assets

All fonts are self-hosted. No Google Fonts or other external font CDNs are used. No third-party scripts are loaded.
